5 SHORT STORIES

The Forgotten Note

In a small town, 16-year-old Mia discovers an old, forgotten note while cleaning out her attic. The note, written in her grandmother's handwriting, is addressed to someone named "Ethan" and speaks of a long-lost love and a secret that was never revealed. Intrigued, Mia sets out to uncover the truth behind the note, discovering more about her grandmother's past and the person she once was. Along the way, Mia learns about the complexities of love and family, and finds herself growing closer to her own future.
